AVA Technologies Development high-tech software

The company was established in 2022, and since its inception, we have acquired valuable experience through collaborations with research and development firms, effectively delivering projects across various industries.
We've formed a team consisting of exceptionally skilled developers and scientists, each possessing strong backgrounds in engineering, mathematics, algorithms, and GPU expertise.

Currently, our primary focus is directed towards the development of a service designed to process and analyze images obtained from scanning electron microscopes (SEM).

The objective of this project is to develop universal software for the automatic analysis and processing of SEM data, independent of the microscope manufacturer and shooting modes, while extending the range of analysis methods to encompass a broad spectrum of industries and imaging modes (e.g., TEM, BSE, X-ray, etc.).
